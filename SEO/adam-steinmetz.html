Adam Steinmetz’s practice focuses on patent infringement litigation and IP counseling.  He has represented and counseled clients in disputes related to a variety of technologies and industries, including pharmaceuticals, medical devices, electronics, and energy.  Prior to joining Desmarais, Adam worked as a clerk for a District Court Judge in the District of New Jersey and before that as an associate in the IP Litigation group at Ropes & Gray.
