Peter Magic's practice focuses on patent infringement litigation. He has represented plaintiffs and defendants in a variety of industries including computer software, hardware, online gaming, telecommunications, cell phone technology and medical devices. He has extensive experience in all stages of litigation, including trials in Federal District Court, the International Trade Commission, and Europe, as well as appeals. Before joining SomeCompany, Mr. Magic was an associate in the Intellectual Property group at Kirkland & Ellis LLP.
